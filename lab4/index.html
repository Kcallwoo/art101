Ristretto Coffee Steps

We made a general list of steps necessary to make a Ristretoo coffee, one of the most difficult coffees to make. This is a relatively short list to help beginners:


							
     
								
  1. Use 18 grams of espresso roast coffee for a double shot 
    2. 
								
  2.  Grind the coffee until very fine
    3. 
								
  3.  Add the coffee grounds to an espresso basket (portafilter) until heaping over the top
    4. 
								
  4.  Place the portafilter in a folded towel
    5. 
								
  5. Use a tamper to firmly press grounds evenly into the portafilter
    6. 
								
  6.  Ensure coffee grounds are as even as possible 
    7. 
								
  7.  Place the portafilter in the espresso machine 
    8. 
								
  8. Press the button to pull the shot using Manually Mode
    9. 
								
  9. Make sure it lasts 15 to 20 seconds and results in 1 1/2 ounces of coffee with a cream-colored crema
    10.
